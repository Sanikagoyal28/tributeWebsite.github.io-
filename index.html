A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam 115 October 1931 _ 27 July 2015 was an Indian Aerospace scientist who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram , Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering . He spent the next four decades as a scientist and science administrator , mainly at the Defence Research and Developement Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) and was intimately inc=volved in India's civilian soace programme and military missile developement efforts. [1] He thus came to be known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the developement of ballistic missile amd launch vehicle technology. [2][3][4] He also played a pivotal organizational, technical, and political role im India's Pokhran - 2nd nuclear tests in 1998, the firsrt since the original nuclear test by India in 1974.
